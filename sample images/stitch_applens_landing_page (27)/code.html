How AppLens Works

Understand the journey from raw data to actionable AI-powered insights in minutes.

Overview

AppLens is designed for simplicity and power. It treats each of your datasets as an independent, self-contained unit. This means you can connect disparate data sources without worrying about complex integrations or data warehousing. AppLens handles the heavy lifting, allowing you to focus on uncovering insights.

Dynamic Schema Handling

Forget rigid schemas. AppLens intelligently adapts to your data's structure, whether it's a well-organized database or a messy CSV. It automatically detects columns, data types, and relationships, saving you hours of manual configuration and data preparation.
